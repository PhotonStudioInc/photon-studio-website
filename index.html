FAQ

What happens after drop‑off or mail‑in?

We perform safe diagnostics and email a short report with a fixed quote. Approve to proceed; otherwise only the $60 deposit applies.

Do you charge if nothing is recovered?

No. You only pay the diagnostic deposit. If we can’t recover, there’s no additional fee.

Can you work with encrypted drives (BitLocker, FileVault/APFS)?

Yes, with your password or recovery key. Without it, modern encryption generally prevents recovery.

My drive clicks or isn’t detected — what should I do?

Power it off and avoid repeated attempts. Bring or ship it to us — we’ll assess and image it safely.

Do you open drives (cleanroom work)?

We start with non‑intrusive methods and imaging. If cleanroom procedures are required, we’ll explain risks and quote options first.

Can you recover camera, drone, or CCTV footage?

Yes. We handle fragmented video and many proprietary containers common to action cams, drones, dashcams, and CCTV.

How is my data returned?

We transfer recovered files to your provided drive or supply one at cost. We retain a secure copy for 7 days, then verify‑wipe.

Do you offer discounts?

Yes, we support students and local small businesses on a case‑by‑case basis. Ask during diagnostics.

Is remote recovery possible?

Yes, for logical cases. If you can provide a dd/Clonezilla image, we can analyze and recover from the clone safely.
